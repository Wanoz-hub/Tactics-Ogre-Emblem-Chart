Character Classes

A guide to all classes in The Knight of Lodis.

Male Classes

Class Requirements Benefits
Soldier None The basic starting class. Balanced stats and can use a variety of swords, armor, and shields.
Ninja STR 36, AGI 37 High agility, can traverse water. Excels with katanas and has the "Shuriken" ranged ability.
Archer AGI 37 Specialized ranged attackers who excel with bows, keeping them out of immediate danger.
Wizard MP 16, INT 26, Alignment: N or C Offensive magic-users with strong INT and MP growth. Cast powerful area-of-effect spells.
Cleric MP 18, INT 28, Alignment: L or N The primary healing class, essential for sustaining your army with healing and support spells.
Knight STR 51, AGI 53, Alignment: L or N, Emblem: Knight's Certificate Sturdy front-line fighters with good STR/DEF. Can use powerful gear and low-level healing magic.
Swordmaster STR 95, AGI 111, Alignment: L or N, Emblem: Book of Initiation Highly skilled swordsmen with exceptional agility. Adept with katanas and powerful special attacks.
Dragoon HP 215, STR 109, AGI 94, Emblem: Dragon's Scale Elite warriors specialized in slaying dragons and beasts. High HP and STR.
Warlock MP 78, STR 90, INT 76, Alignment: N or C, Emblem: The Pen and the Sword Hybrid class combining physical combat with powerful magic. Can equip swords and use offensive spells.
Beast Tamer STR 34 Specialized in recruiting and commanding beasts. Gains a persuasion bonus against beasts and can use whips.
Priest MP 76, INT 96, Alignment: L or N, Emblem: Heavenly Spirit Advanced healing class with access to the most powerful restorative and support magic in the game.

Female Classes

Class Requirements Benefits
Amazon None The basic starting class for female recruits with balanced stats.
Archer AGI 37 Proficient with bows to provide excellent ranged support.
Valkyrie STR 48, INT 41, Alignment: L or N, Emblem: Lancer Versatile warriors proficient in both physical combat (spears) and offensive magic.
Wizard MP 16, INT 26, Alignment: N or C Female Wizards share the same role as males, wielding powerful offensive magic from the back lines.
Cleric MP 18, INT 28, Alignment: L or N Female Clerics are dedicated healers, crucial for keeping your army alive.
Siren MP 60, INT 60, Emblem: Philosopher's Stone Powerful offensive spellcasters who can use some of the most devastating magic, including summon spells.
Witch MP 38, INT 35, Alignment: N or C, Emblem: Vixen's Whisper Specialists in debilitating status-effect magic (charm, sleep, poison).
Dragon Tamer HP 140, MP 14, STR 35, AGI 42 Dedicated to recruiting and commanding dragons. Gains a persuasion bonus against them.
Priest MP 76, INT 96, Alignment: L or N, Emblem: Heavenly Spirit Advanced healing class with access to the most powerful restorative and support magic in the game.

Special & Demi-Human Classes

Class How to Acquire Benefits
Hawkman Recruited in battle. Flying units with excellent mobility that can ignore terrain obstacles. Strong physical attackers.
Mermaid Recruited in battle. Aquatic units that excel in water, gaining increased movement and combat effectiveness.
Fairy Recruited in battle. Small, flying units. Low combat stats but can use support abilities and are difficult to hit.
Giant Recruited in battle. Large and powerful units with immense STR and HP. Slow but can deal devastating physical damage.
Angel Knight Have a Lawful character with the Archangel's Feather emblem die in battle. They may be reborn. A powerful, flying holy warrior with high all-around stats and unique abilities to smite evil.
Lich Have a powerful Chaotic magic-user die while equipped with the Ring of the Dead. Undead master of magic with access to forbidden spells. Resurrects after being struck down.
